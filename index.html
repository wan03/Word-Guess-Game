Wan's Hangman Game

Hi! Welcome to my game. The rules are very simple:


  1. The computer will chose a random word when you click New Game.
  2. You can guess a letter by pressing a letter in your keyboard.
  3. If its part of the word, that letter will be revealed if it is not part of the word, you will lose a life.
  4. If you guess the word correctly you will win and receive a reward but if you run out of lives you will lose.
  5. The theme is Japanese Martial Arts
  6. Have fun!
