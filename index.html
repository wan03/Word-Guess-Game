Wan's Hangman Game

Hi! Welcome to my game. The rules are very simple:

  1. The computer will chose a random word
  2. You can guess a letter
  3. If its part of the word that letter will be revealed if it is not part of the word you lose a life
  4. If you guess the word corrently you will be awarded a point if you don't your lose counter will increase
  5. Have fun!
